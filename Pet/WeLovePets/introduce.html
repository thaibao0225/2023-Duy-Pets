Introduction

The economy is growing, people's lives are also upgraded and improved. Therefore, the demand for pets has also increased significantly. According to statistics, the pet industry will explode in many countries within 5 years next. Including Vietnam.

Understanding this, WeLovePets.com has been constantly trying to become the most prestigious pet trading brand in Vietnam. We are one of the pioneers in the application of modern technology the field of pet care & trading.

Activities

  • Buy and sell pets

    WeLovePets has a staff of experienced and professional pet breeders. Therefore, the animals born at the WeLovePets camp are always the best quality babies with beautiful appearance. When buying and selling animals dear, you can go to the hatchery to check and choose with your own eyes.

    In addition, we are also developing a nationwide pet trading system. Divided into 2 operations: Buying pets & selling pets. WeLovePets' mission is to be an intermediary to ensure the quality of pets before delivery to customers.

  • Buying a pet

    We cooperate with many large and small dog farms and prestigious households across the country. If you have a pet for sale, please contact us. After that, WeLovePets staff will come to survey and negotiate directly if the pet is purchased yours is of good quality.

    The requirements of WeLovePets.com when cooperating to buy pets are:

    • Your puppy must be fully vaccinated against diseases.
    • Good health, no birth defects.
    • The dog is agile, energetic, and has a well-balanced appearance.

  • Selling pets

    When buying pets at WeLovePets, in addition to ensuring that the pets you choose are of good quality, we also have many preferential policies for customers such as:

    • WeLovePets is committed to always offering the best and most reasonable prices for customers.
    • Provide photos, videos and real information about puppies for customers. Guaranteed to work with prestige and responsibility, taking customer satisfaction as a goal to strive for.
    • Currently, WeLovePets.com owns all pet lines available on the Vietnamese market. So just leave your order information, we will contact and meet your needs.
    • Your puppy will be fully vaccinated against diseases, guaranteed health, good breed quality.

  • Pet care

    When do you need to use Petshop's pet care service? That's when you:

    • Going away for work, traveling far away, unable to take care of her pet for a long period of time. At this time, you absolutely must not leave the puppy, but bring them to our WeLovePets.
    • You want to find an ideal entertainment environment for your pet.
    • You don't know how to take care of a newborn pet. Or due to many other reasons such as: Loss of mother, not enough milk…

    WeLovePets' pet care service will ensure to meet all of the above problems of customers. We have an ideal and scientific living environment for pets. Here, they will undergo a thorough care process biblical, scientific. Including: Personal hygiene, feeding, exercise, training…

    For newborn puppies, WeLovePets has 2 scientific raising methods: Set feeding (breeding newborn puppies completely without using breast milk) or grafting. Your puppy will be cared for, fed, and fully vaccinated. After 2 months, we The pet will be returned to you and a service fee will be collected.

  • Imported dog

    Foreign dogs are always the first choice of customers when coming to WeLovePets. Why is that? Because imported dogs possess many outstanding advantages compared to ordinary ornamental dogs in terms of appearance, health, breed quality, and personality..

    The source of Petshop's foreign dogs comes from countries with a developed pet industry such as: Thailand, UK, France, USA, .. and many other countries in Europe and America. When buying, customers can choose the source of imported pets. Then they I will respond and provide information, quotation for you.

    Are you worried about the quality of imported dogs? WeLovePets would like to assure you that, before being delivered to customers, foreign dogs have gone through extremely strict and strict quarantine stages. First is the quarantine of the camp foreign dog. Then there is the exit quarantine, the entry quarantine. And finally the WeLovePets quarantine.

    Therefore, we are confident & committed to our customers about the quality and purity of the puppies you choose at Petshop.
